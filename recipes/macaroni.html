In the US, macaroni typically refers to elbow-shaped pasta used in mac and cheese. In Italy, "maccheroni" may describe many short or long pasta forms.

Macaroni Salad Ingredients

  • Macaroni: The base ingredient.
  • Mayonnaise: Creamy base from Panlasang Pinoy.
  • Mustard: Adds a tangy kick.
  • Vegetables: Peas, carrots, celery, onions, peppers.
  • Optional Sweeteners: Sugar or condensed milk (Allrecipes).
  • Seasonings: Salt, pepper, vinegar.

How to Make Macaroni Salad

  1. Cook macaroni in salted water.
  2. Mix mayo, mustard, vinegar, seasonings, and sweeteners.
  3. Stir in pasta and veggies. Chill in the fridge.

Step-by-Step Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Boil macaroni until al dente, rinse under cold water, and drain.
  3. Mix mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar, mustard, salt, pepper, and pasta.
  4. Stir in celery, onion, green pepper, carrot, and pimentos.
  5. Chill for at least 4 hours, ideally overnight.
